Here are some must-know facts about Liverpool

Facts


Anfield was orginally Everton's stadium however over a rent dispute it was given to Liverpool and Everton had to move to Goodison Park

Liverpool has won the European Cup/UEFA Champions League more than any other team in England

When John Houlding was the club's president, he signed 13 Scottish players

Liverpool's anthem is You'll Never Walk Alone

It is the most successful English club in European tournament

The first Liverpool football club was established in 1857 however they are not associated with the current club

In the first season for Liverpool, they had made it to the second division without being defeated. They also won against Newton Heath 2-0, who is now known as Manchester United.

The Kop is named after the Spion Kop, a hill in Africa where the Boer War in January 1900 left hundreds dead.
