About the Reds


Liverpool Football Club is amongst one of the best and most successful football clubs to have ever graced the sport. It is based in Liverpool, Merseyside and it is well known for its historical comebacks, amazing achievements and much more. Established in 1892 by John Houlding, the former owner of the now famous stadium Anfield. It is currently the most successful team in England, conquering 19 English league titles, 6 Uefa Champions Leagues, and 8 FA Cups. Known for its electric and passionate fanbase, their home games are played at Anfield, a stadium famed for the “Kop” stand, filled with an electrifying and magnetic atmosphere. On top of all that, Liverpool is home to a vast amount of history and historical players. These players include Sir Kenny Dalglish, Steven Gerrard, Mohammad Salah and many more talents. It has also been a dominant force in European football, especially under legendary managers such as Bill Shankly, Bob Paisley and Jurgen Klopp.
