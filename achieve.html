Being a club filled with great history, Liverpool have racked up a total of 51 Major Trophies since it was established in 1892

19 League Titles


1900-01, 1905-06, 1921-22, 1922-23, 1946-47, 1963-64, 1965-66, 1972-73, 1975-76, 1976-77, 1978-79, 1979-80, 1981-82, 1982-83, 1983-84, 1985-86, 1987-88, 1989-90, 2019-20


Now known as the Premier League, Liverpool have won a remarkable 19 League Titles. The most recent of which came from German manager, Jurgen Klopp during the 2019-20 season.

6 European Cups


1976-77, 1977-78, 1980-81, 1983-84, 2004-05, 2018-19


Now called the UEFA Champions League, Liverpool have won a stunning 6 European Cups. First 3 came under Bob Paisley, followed by Joe Fagan. During the 2005 season, manager Rafael Benitez led the team to the succession of the Miracle of Istanbul, shattering Milan's dreams. Making a 3 goal comeback against one of Milan’s best teams ever. However, after a dramatic comeback in the Semis, Jurgen Klopp led the team to its 6th European Cup in 2019.

Club World Cup


2019


In 2019, Liverpool had added a new achievement to their collection, a Club World Cup. Led by the legendary, Jurgen Klopp.

8 FA Cups


1964-65, 1973-74, 1985-86, 1988-89, 1991-92, 2000-01, 2005-06, 2021-22


Liverpool have won the FA Cup 8 times, with the first in 1965 under Bill Shankly and the most recent in 2022 under Jurgen Klopp

10 League Cups


1980-81, 1981-82, 1982-83, 1983-84, 1994-95, 2000-01, 2002-03, 2011-12, 2021-22, 2023-24


Liverpool have won the League Cup 10 times, the most recent of which being in 2024, against Chelsea.

3 UEFA Cups


1972-73, 1975-76, 2000-01


Liverpool have won the UEFA Cup 3 times. Each under different managers, Bill Shankly, Bob Paisley and Gerard Houllier.

4 UEFA Super Cups


1977, 2001, 2005, 2019


Liverpool have won the UEFA Super Cup 4 times, 3 of Liverpool’s European Cup and 1 of the UEFA Cup had led to these victories.
