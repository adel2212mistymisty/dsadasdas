The Academy/Future


Filled with young talents from all ages, Liverpool’s academy has always been beneficial and crucial towards the club’s success. Providing elite coaches for players playing in the U9 all the way to U21 categories. Many prospects have made it to the senior team, for example, Trent Alexander-Arnold, and have thrived since their graduation from the academy. The academy continues to grow a better and brighter future for Liverpool and they will surely produce generational talents which will make a name for themselves in the upcoming years.
